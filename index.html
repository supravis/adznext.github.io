About Us

Adznext is a performance advertising and marketing agency catering to advertisers' media plans and helping them with technology driven varied marketing solutions.

With an expert team with in-depth experience and knowledge of the advertising industry and a global publisher network,we help advertisers to increase user acquisition scale, engagement, retention and revenues through smart in-app advertising.

We offer different acquisition models like CPM, CPI, CPA, CPT to get the best ROI for campaigns across verticals like E-commerce, Gaming, Social, Utility, Education, Fintech and more. We have technology across multiple digital platforms to study and evaluate the user experience and design strategy. Adznext supports an array of creative formats like banners, interstitial, videos, social and native ads.
